About Us

Dr. Khail Awad is a gentle caring and highly experienced chiropractor. He specializes in spinal correction, family wellness, spinal decompression and educating his patients in proper spinal health care.

Dr. Awad has been in practice for over 25 years and has treated patients with chronic or acute back, neck, shoulder, sciatic nerve and extremity pain. As a chiropractor, he believes in natural healing; getting to the source of the problem and helps the body to self-correct.

Dr. Awad is committed to patient oriented care. His goal is to “heal as many sick people as possible”. He offers Chiropractic care, Spinal Decompression, Physical Therapy and Rehab. Dr. Awad also has Dynamic Motion X-ray on-site.
